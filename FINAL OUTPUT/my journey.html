My Journey

  • When I began my career in graphic design
    (about 8 years ago), I started as a part-time Graphic artist,
    having come from a multimedia background and with minimal
    word-processing experience let alone graphics skills.
    I treated such different softwares as places I aspire to
    explore and be familiar with until it came into the time
    where I got used to the directions and carved my own path
    towards success.
  • Not only that, I was not limited to just one kind of media.
    I also immersed in video editing, having from a simple Video Pad or
    an online website that only cuts and compiles clips up to a much
    more advance software to work on, expanding creative features
    that maximize my work of art through moving pictures, editing
    songs, and so much more.
  • My name is Van Humphrey.
    This is my journey.
